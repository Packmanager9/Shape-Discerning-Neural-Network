Competitive Neural Networks

  • 400 neural networks compete to classify the same data.
  • The top left square is the color assigned to the fittest neural network.
  • From left-to-right and top-to-bottom they are ranked by their fitness.
  • Open the console to see more.
  • Pressing 'f' will log out all of the current neural networks.
  • The fittest neural network will be used to produce new neural networks of a similar color every generation.
  • The penalty for incorrect classification rises with time.
